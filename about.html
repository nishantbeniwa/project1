About Us

We are a vibrant and close-knit community of people belonging to the esteemed "Chauseni Vaish Samaj," also known by various names such as "Chyavan Vaish," "Chausaini Baniya," and "Chaturshringi Vaish." Our roots are deeply embedded in the reverence of Chyavan Rishi, the revered sage who bestowed the world with the invaluable gift of Chyavanprash.

Our Heritage


Our community holds a rich heritage, tracing its origins to Chyavan Rishi, the sage credited with inventing Chyavanprash, a renowned Ayurvedic formulation known for its health benefits. We take pride in preserving and promoting our cultural heritage, passing down our traditions from generation to generation.


Community Presence


he families of the Chauseni Vaish Samaj are spread across various regions, with significant populations residing in Lohwan (Mathura), Khurja, Bulandshahar, Aligarh, New Delhi, Gwalior, and other parts of the world. We embrace the diversity of our community and aim to foster unity and harmony among our members, regardless of geographic location.


Our Objectives


Our primary objective is to unite and connect the people of the Chauseni Vaish Samaj, irrespective of their global locations. Through this platform, we strive to create a strong sense of community, nurture relationships, and work towards the betterment and well-being of our members and society at large.


Get Involved


OWe invite all members of the Chauseni Vaish Samaj to actively participate in our initiatives, events, and charitable activities. Together, we can strengthen our community bonds, celebrate our heritage, and contribute to a brighter future for all.


For more information, updates, and to become a part of our vibrant community, please explore our website and stay connected.
